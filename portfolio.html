About Me

Shant is a hands-on, results oriented professional equipped with 17 years of IT and business related success, experienced in software and database development, business systems analysis, project management and ERP consultancy. Shant received his BS in Computer Science in 1999 (LAU, Beirut). After working for couple of years as a sofftware developer in Middle east and Europe Shant, advanced his education with MBA in management in 2010 (LAU, Beirut).

Mr. Shant moved to US in 2013 and he is currently working at one of well known restaurants chain corporations in California as an "Enterprise Systems Manager".
